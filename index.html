MOROCCO

Read a little bit about this magnificent country !

Morocco, officially the Kingdom of Morocco, is the northwesternmost country in the Maghreb region of North Africa. It overlooks the Mediterranean Sea to the north and the Atlantic Ocean to the west, and has land borders with Algeria to the east, and the disputed territory of Western Sahara to the south. Morocco also claims the Spanish exclaves of Ceuta, Melilla and Peñón de Vélez de la Gomera, and several small Spanish-controlled islands off its coast.

CASABLANCA

the heart of Morocco

Arteries bordered by palm trees, large business buildings and the Atlantic that opens up to the world: Casablanca is the economic heart of Morocco, a bustling metropolis constantly on the alert.

Read more !

RABAT

the capital of Morocco

Rabat is a cultural city steeped in history. Many masterpieces decorate its streets and squares. Visit the Kasbah des Oudayas: majestic and formidable silhouette softened by the surrounding gardens. Not far from the walls rise the walls of Chellah, a necropolis from the time of the Merinids. To cross the enclosure, it is to enter another world: you walk in the middle of ancient vestiges, among the gardens and the storks.

Read more !

FES

the mother of Morocco

Fez radiates a singular aura. It is the Imperial, the depository of thirteen centuries of Moroccan history. Fez has a rich past and has unexpected surprises in store.

Here is what you can do in Morocco :

Horse riding

An ancient heritage

Morocco is the heir to a long tradition illustrated by the national stud farms of Marrakech, Meknes, Oujda, El Jadida or Bouznika. During your stay or your trip, ride a horse for an obstacle course or for a leisurely hike, along the beach or in the Moroccan hinterland.

more information...

Cycling

Cycle trips in the heart of nature

Another mount, but still so much fun! Cycling is also in the spotlight in the Atlas Mountains. Around Agadir, Marrakech, routes unfold in the middle of national parks crowned with mountains like that of Talassemtane and present mountain bikers with some major challenges!

more information...

Caving

For the pleasure of discovery

Fans of more adventurous outings? Discover caving! Morocco is dotted with caves, each more spectacular than the next, like those of Hercules in Tangier. In the Middle Atlas, it is the site of Kehf Tikhoubai that deserves the detour: its galleries plunge into the bowels of the earth to nearly 310 meters deep.

Food and drinks :

Renowned for its delectable and various dishes, Morocco was classified second in the 2017 ranking of the best gourmand's global destinations, according to the British blog Worldsim Travel. The kingdom offers to its visitors an exceptional culinary experience. Marrakech, Fez, Agadir and Rabat are among the most tempting culinary destinations. More than 15 Moroccan restaurants are amongst the 1000 best restaurants worldwide, according to« La Liste ». Enjoy the best Moroccan culinary delights has to offer to you : Mint tea, Tajine, Couscous, or Pastilla, are awaiting for you !.
